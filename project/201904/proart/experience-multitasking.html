Multitasking Made Easy

ASUS ProArt Series features Thunderbolt™ 3 technology for data transfer, signal, Power Delivery and daisy-chaining through a single port. In addition, Picture-in-Picture (PiP) and Picture-by-Picture (PbP) modes so you can view content from two to four different inputs simultaneously. This helps you fully maximize your display’s onscreen area so you get more done quickly.

Thunderbolt™ 3: The USB-C that does it all

For unrivaled expandability and versatile, ultrafast connections, ProArt series monitor includes two Thunderbolt™ 3 for data transfer speeds of up to 40Gb/s, DisplayPort and USB 3.1 with Power Delivery for providing up to 60W of power to external devices. In addition, you can daisy-chain two 4K displays through a single port without the need for a hub or a switch. With Thunderbolt™ 3, the possibilities are truly endless!

*Specifications may vary by model

Picture by Picture (PbP) mode

View content from two to four inputs side-by-side onscreen. You can even configure each individual window's color settings with Adobe RGB, sRGB, User Pre-set 1, and User Pre-set 2 modes* to see the differences between them.

*Specifications may vary by model

Picture in Picture (PiP) mode

This places your second input source in a window in one corner of the display.
