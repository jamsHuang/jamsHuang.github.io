THE ESSENTIAL TOOL FOR VIDEO PRODUCTION

It’s time to get creative. Trim, mix, play and create engaging content on ASUS ProArt Series monitors.

Perfect colors, every time

ASUS ProArt Series gives you perfect colors in every frame, making it a great monitor for video editing. The high color accuracy provided by ProArt Series monitors also makes it easy when you’re working in a team — because everyone sees the same exact color on the piece of film being worked on. Every monitor has been carefully tested to reduce the Delta E color difference value to less than 2* to ensure precise rendering of input colors and exceptional color fidelity.

*specifications may vary by model

Wide color space: Tell a story through colors

Color plays an integral role in visual storytelling, with the slightest variation in color deeply affecting the emotional impact of a scene. ASUS ProArt Series monitors have a wide color gamut so you have an entire spectrum of colors to play with, it’s a feature that’s especially useful when you’re using it as a video editing monitor. Quantum Dot technology provides a wide color range and supports 100% Rec. 709, 100% sRGB, 99.5% Adobe RGB, 95% DCI-P3, and 85% Rec. 2020 color spaces for video editing and production.

*specifications may vary by model

4K HDR: Lifelike visual experience

ASUS ProArt Series monitors feature 4K UHD (3840 X 2160) panels to give you four times the pixel density and up to 300% more onscreen space than similar-sized Full HD displays, it’s great for multitasking, so you get things done quickly. With Ultra HD Premium certification – the industry standard for HDR. Designed to enhance the contrast between the brightest and darkest parts of an image, HDR technology delivers exceptional onscreen clarity and detail. Featuring a full-array LED backlight with ASUS LED Driving technology, ProArt PA32UC features 384 LED zones and a peak brightness of 1,000 nits, so it can produce the deepest blacks and the brightest whites with sharper, more detailed visuals.

Extensive connectivity for various input sources

Monitors for video editing require extensive connectivity options so you can hook them up to various video input sources, and setup multi-displays for video editing and previewing. ASUS ProArt Series offers two Thunderbolt™ 3 ports for data transfer speeds of up to 40Gbps, DisplayPort and USB 3.1 with Power Delivery for providing up to 60W of power to external devices. You can daisy-chain two 4K monitors through a single port without the need for a hub or a switch. In addition, it also provides DisplayPort, mini DisplayPort 1.2, and HDMI (v2.0)*. In addition, there are USB 3.0 ports and a 9-in-1 card reader* for your memory cards, peripherals and various devices.

*Specifications may vary by model
