THE ESSENTIAL TOOL FOR PHOTOGRAPHY

ASUS ProArt Series is truly a monitor for photographers. The pictures you took are replicated onscreen,
you’ll even get to see how your edited photos will turn out in their final form.

True to life colors

When it comes to photo editing, we know that a well-calibrated monitor with exceptional color reproduction makes all the difference. And that’s why we’ve pre-calibrated each ASUS ProArt Series monitor at the factory to guarantee industry-leading color accuracy. Each monitor has a Delta E color difference value that is less than 2* to ensure exceptional color fidelity and precise rendering of input colors.

This ensures what your clients see is exactly what is shown on your screen.

*actual specifications may vary by model

Wide color space: Bring out the true beauty of your picture

Monitors for photo editing should provide outstanding colors to help bring your photographs to life. ASUS ProArt Series monitors feature a wide color gamut to cover the entire ISO-coated and US web-coated CMYK color spaces used in printing. This ensures that your final images turn out exactly how you want them to be — so expect beautiful photographs with lush green forests or portraits that convey the emotion of your subject.

It offers accurate color space reproduction, while 10-bit display color provides over 1 billion onscreen colors for smoother gradations and a more natural transition between hues.

4K UHD: It’s all in the details

Being able to clearly make out skin textures and individual strands of hair is crucial for all photographers and retouchers. ASUS ProArt Series monitors give you up to 4K UHD 3840 x 2160 resolution visuals for incredible levels of detail — making it the perfect tool when you’re looking to retouch, resize, or cropping the photographs.

Ergonomically-designed for your comfort

ASUS ProArt Series monitors are ergonomically-designed too, with tilt, pivot, swivel and high adjustment so you stay comfortable even after spending long hours in front of the display. They’re also VESA mount-compatible so you have the freedom to pair it with an after-market stand or mount it on your studio wall.
